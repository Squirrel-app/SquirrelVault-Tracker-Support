Summary

  • We only collect the minimum account info from your sign‑in provider (Google, Apple, or Facebook): email, display name, avatar (if any), and a technical account ID.
  • Your inventory and notes are stored locally on your device. We do not upload them to our servers.
  • No ads and no data sales. We do not share personal data with third‑party marketers.

Data We Collect

Account information

When you sign in with Google, Apple, or Facebook, we receive your email, display name, avatar URL (if available), and a technical account ID. This is used only to authenticate you and secure access to the app.

App data (local)

Your items, notes, photos, and reminder settings are kept on your device. Until cloud sync is released, they are not transmitted to us.

Crash logs

If enabled on your device, Apple may provide anonymous crash reports to help us fix bugs. These reports are handled by Apple and may be shared with us; they do not include your inventory content.

How We Use Data

  • Authenticate your account and keep sessions secure.
  • Send notifications you opt in to (e.g., expiry reminders).
  • Improve the app’s reliability and performance.

We do not sell your data. We do not read your email or contacts.

Sharing

We do not share personal data with third‑party advertisers or data brokers. Service providers only process data on our behalf as needed to operate the App.

  • Google — authentication (Google Sign‑In).
  • Apple — Sign in with Apple; anonymous crash reports (if you opted in on your device).
  • Meta (Facebook) — authentication (Facebook Login).

We may disclose information if required by law or legal process, or to protect our rights, safety, or property. We may use anonymized, aggregated data for analytics and product improvement that does not identify you.

Retention

Account info is kept while your account is active. Local app data (stored in iOS using Core Data) stays on your device until you delete it, use in‑app clear options, or uninstall the App. We do not maintain server backups of your inventory.

Your Rights

Depending on your region (EU/EEA/UK/Canada/USA), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your data, and to withdraw consent where applicable. To exercise these rights, contact us. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Portuguese data protection authority (CNPD).

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children.
