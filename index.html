About the app

SquirrelVault Tracker is a fast, minimal inventory & expiry tracker for home and work. It helps you stay on top of pantry products and especially medicine & vitamins — the items whose expiry dates people most often miss.

  • Smart reminders before items expire
  • Fast capture with notes, photos, and barcode scan
  • Export full list as PDF for printing or sharing
  • Privacy by default — items and notes are stored locally on your device

Google sign-in is required for secure access and upcoming features (cloud storage, shared vaults). The app is free; there are no in-app purchases at the moment.

Quick Start

  • Sign in with Google: required at first launch for secure access.
  • Add your first item: Home → “+” → name, quantity, expiry date (or use Scan barcode).
  • Delete: swipe left on an item → Delete.
  • Export inventory (PDF): Profile → Share (PDF) → Save or Share.
  • Enable notifications: Profile → Notifications. If needed, iOS Settings → Notifications → SquirrelVault Tracker → Allow.
  • Clear all local data: Profile → Clear All Storage (permanent).

FAQs

Is the app free? Are there in-app purchases?

The app is free right now. There are no in-app purchases at the moment. If pricing changes, we’ll announce it in the app and update this page.

Do I need an account? Why Google sign-in?

Yes. Google sign-in is required for authentication, secure access, and upcoming features like cloud storage and shared vaults. We only receive your basic Google profile (email, display name, avatar if any, and a technical account ID) for sign-in and security.

Do you collect personal data or send my inventory to servers?

We don’t read your email or access your contacts. Your inventory and notes are currently not sent to our servers and stay on your device. See the Privacy Policy for details.

How do I add items quickly?

Tap + on Home to add an item in seconds. Use Scan barcode to speed up input.

How do I delete an item?

Swipe left on the item and tap Delete.

How do I export my list?

Open the app → Profile → Share (PDF) → Save or Share. You’ll get a single PDF with your full list.

Where are my data stored? Is there cloud sync?

Your inventory is stored locally on your device. Cloud sync is not available yet — we’ll update this page when it’s ready.

Will I lose my data if I delete the app or switch phones?

Until cloud sync arrives, data live locally. If you delete the app or move to a new device without restoring an iOS backup, your data won’t transfer.

What kinds of items is this best for?

Pantry products, medicine & vitamins, cosmetics, and household supplies.
