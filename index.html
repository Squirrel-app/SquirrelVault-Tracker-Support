About the app

SquirrelVault Tracker is a fast, minimal inventory & expiry tracker for home and work. It helps you keep items organized, avoid waste, and stay on top of what expires soon.

  • Smart reminders before items expire
  • Fast capture with notes and photos
  • Barcode scan to speed up adding items
  • Export full product list as PNG for printing or sharing
  • Privacy by default — data are stored locally on your device

Cloud sync via iCloud — coming soon.

Quick Start

  • Add your first item: Home → “+” → name, quantity, expiry date (or use Scan barcode).
  • Delete: swipe left on an item → Delete.
  • Export inventory (PNG): Profile → Export → Inventory (PNG) → Share or Save.
  • Enable notifications: Profile → Notifications. If needed, in iOS: Settings → Notifications → SquirrelVault Tracker → Allow.
  • Clear all local data: Profile → Clear All Storage (permanent).

FAQs

Is the app free? Are there purchases?

There are no in-app purchases at the moment. If pricing changes in the future, we’ll announce it in the app and update this page.

How do I export my inventory as PNG?

Open the app → Profile → Export → choose Inventory (PNG) → Share or Save. You’ll get a single PNG with your product list.

How do I delete an item?

In any list, swipe left on the item → tap Delete.

How do notifications work?

In the app: Profile → Notifications — turn on reminders and set the time.
In iOS: Settings → Notifications → SquirrelVault Tracker — allow notifications.

Where are my data stored? Is there cloud sync?

Your data are stored locally on your device. Cloud sync is not available yet — we’re working on it and will update this page when it’s ready.

How do I contact support from the app?

Profile → Contact Support. This opens an email draft to our support address; you can attach screenshots or a short video.
