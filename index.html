FAQ

Is the app free? Are there purchases?

There are no in-app purchases at the moment. We’ll announce changes in the app and update this page.

How to export my inventory as PNG?

Open the app → Profile → Export → choose Inventory (PNG) → Share or Save. You’ll get a single PNG with your product list for easy sharing or printing.

How to delete an item?

In any list, swipe left on the item → tap Delete. You can also use Edit to modify name, quantity or notes.

How to enable notifications?

In the app: Profile → Notifications — enable reminders and set the time.

In iOS settings (if needed): Settings → Notifications → SquirrelVault Tracker — allow notifications.

Where are my data stored? Cloud sync?

Your data are stored locally on your device. Cloud sync is not available yet — we’re working on it and will update this page when it’s ready.

How to clear all local data?

Profile → Clear All Storage. This permanently removes local data from the device.

How to contact support from the app?

Profile → Contact Support. This opens an email draft to our support address; you can attach screenshots or a short video.
