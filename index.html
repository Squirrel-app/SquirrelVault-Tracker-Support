About

SquirrelVault Tracker is a fast, minimal inventory & expiry tracker. It helps you stay on top of pantry products and especially medicine & vitamins — the items whose expiry dates are easy to miss.

  • Smart reminders before items expire
  • Fast capture with notes, photos, and barcode scan
  • Export your list as a single PDF
  • Privacy by default — items & notes are stored locally on your device

Google sign‑in is required for authentication and upcoming features (cloud storage, shared vaults). The app is free; there are no in‑app purchases at the moment.

Quick Start

  • Sign in: use Google at first launch.
  • Add item: Home → “+” → name, quantity, expiry (or Scan barcode).
  • Delete: swipe left on an item → Delete.
  • Export (PDF): Profile → Share (PDF) → Save or Share.
  • Notifications: Profile → Notifications (enable in iOS Settings if needed).
  • Clear all local data: Profile → Clear All Storage (permanent).

FAQs

Is the app free? Any in‑app purchases?

The app is free right now. There are no in‑app purchases at the moment. If this changes, we’ll update this page and the app.

Do I need an account? Why Google sign‑in?

Yes. Google sign‑in is required for secure access and future features (cloud sync, shared vaults). We receive only basic profile info from the provider (email, display name, avatar if any, and a technical account ID) for authentication.

Is my inventory uploaded to servers?

Your items and notes are stored locally on your device. They are not uploaded to our servers. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Will I lose data if I delete the app or switch phones?

Until cloud sync is available, data live locally. Deleting the app or moving to a new device without restoring an iOS backup will not transfer your data.

How do I request account deletion?

Email squirrelvault.tracker@gmail.com from the address used to sign in and request account deletion. After confirmation, all authentication data we store will be erased. Local items on your device remain under your control; delete them in the app or uninstall the app.
