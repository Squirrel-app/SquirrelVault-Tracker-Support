About

SquirrelVault Tracker is a fast, minimal inventory & expiry tracker. It helps you stay on top of pantry products and especially medicine & vitamins — the items whose expiry dates are easy to miss.

  • Smart reminders before items expire
  • Fast capture with notes and barcode scan
  • AI scan (optional) to help autofill item details
  • Export your list as a single PDF
  • Privacy by design — we aim to collect only what’s needed to run the app

Sign-in may be required for secure access and cloud features. Some advanced features may require a subscription. See Terms and Privacy Policy for details.

Quick Start

  • Sign in: use the sign-in option shown on first launch.
  • Add item: Home → “+” → name, expiry (or Scan).
  • Delete: swipe left on an item → Delete.
  • Export (PDF): Profile → Share (PDF) → Save or Share.
  • Notifications: Profile → Notifications (enable in iOS Settings if needed).
  • Clear data: Profile → Clear All Storage (permanent).

FAQs

Is the app free? Any subscriptions?

The app may offer optional subscriptions (for example, “Pro”) that unlock extra features or higher limits. Availability and pricing are shown in the app at the time of purchase.

Do I need an account? Why sign-in?

Sign-in helps secure access and enables cloud features. We receive only basic profile info from the provider (email, display name, avatar if any, and a technical account ID) for authentication.

Is my inventory uploaded to servers?

When cloud features are enabled, your inventory items and notes may be stored in the cloud so they can be associated with your account. Photos are not uploaded unless explicitly stated in the app. See the Privacy Policy for details.

What is AI scan and what data is sent?

AI scan can help autofill item details based on text recognized from packaging (OCR). If you choose to use AI scan, recognized text may be sent for processing. AI output may be inaccurate; please verify important details (especially medicines). Limits may apply depending on your plan.

Will I lose data if I delete the app or switch phones?

Local app data may be lost if you uninstall the app. If cloud features are enabled, your account-linked data can be restored after you sign in again. Availability depends on your current app version and feature settings.

How do I request account deletion?

Email squirrelvault.tracker@gmail.com from the address used to sign in and request account deletion. After confirmation, we will remove your account-linked cloud data where applicable. Local data on your device remains under your control; delete it in the app or uninstall the app.
